# Marketing scan — Maude + LokNet (Bard Software)

Status: First-pass, 2026-06-10. Linda (gpt-oss-120b) ran the scan; Jason added synthesis.
Pairs with `POSITIONING.md`, `VISION.md`, and claudeTalk `MARKET_SCAN.md` (competition).

## Headlines (Jason's read of Linda's scan)
1. **Rename LokNet — now a two-source recommendation.** Linda independently flagged the
   botnet pun as a *trust liability* for a security brand (matching the earlier POSITIONING
   flag). This has moved from "watch-item" to "decide." "LokNet **Hub**" softens it but the
   bare name still reads as mischief to the exact buyer who must trust it. Candidates below.
2. **Lead with zero-config + on-device; values second.** The #1 adoption killer is friction
   (Tailscale's whole pitch is "zero-config"). Lead with what it *does* and how *easy* it is;
   the founding principle is the soul but **not the lead message** — "don't be preachy, don't
   bury the tech under ideology" (Linda). Our hosted-Hub-by-default onboarding IS the
   zero-config story — market it that way.
3. **The beachhead is reachable for ~$0.** Homelab/self-host/privacy communities congregate
   in known places — no paid UA needed to start.

## Taglines (strongest picks)

## Steal-from-competitors (one lesson each)
- **Tailscale:** lead with **zero-config** (kill friction).
- **Signal:** own the **"your data, your rules"** language.
- **Ollama:** ship a **bundled demo** so entry is one tap (we have the Demo backend — use it).
- **Home Assistant:** build the **plugin/community ecosystem** as the moat (our #66/#68 play).

## Do / Don't (privacy+ownership brand)
Do: "your hardware, your data, your AI"; concrete verbs; **qualify** claims ("by default");
1-tap self-host; privacy FAQ on every page. Don't: overpromise "VPN"/anonymity; "cloud-killer"
(combative, dates fast); "zero-trust" everywhere (eye-glaze); preachy "big tech bad"; tech buried
under ideology.

## Naming decision — for Eddie
Two independent recommendations to rename "LokNet." Linda's candidates: **OwnGate, VaultMesh,
PrivNet, SecureFabric.** Jason's read: "OwnGate" leans ownership (on-brand with the founding
principle) but sounds enterprise; the bare "Hub" framing could also just stand alone
("the Hub" / a fresh coined name). **This is a founder call — flagged, not decided.** Keep
"LokNet" internal until chosen.
